Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

A favourite Korean street food, a wonderful combination of fried rice, hotdog slices and of course, Kimchi!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs, beaten
  • 3 Garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 3cm fresh root Ginger, peeled and finely chopped
  • 3 Frankfurters, sliced
  • 100g Kimchi, roughly chopped
  • 1 or more spring onions, sliced
  • A hanful of frozen garden peas
  • 100g cooked and cooled rice. Basmati, Korean or Jasmine works best.
  • 1tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2.5tsp toasted sesame oil
  • sesame seeds, to garnish

For the spicy sauce

  • 1tbsp Gochujang
  • 1tbsp Kimchi pickling liquid
  • 0.5tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1tbsp light soy sauce
  • 0.25tsp palm sugar

Method

  1. Prepare all ingredients beforehand as this dish is cooked quickly
  2. Make the sauce by mixing all the ingredients together in a small bowl
  3. Heat the oil and when smoking hot pour in the beaten egg and swirl. Stir-fry for 1-2 mins unitl crisp but not burnt
  4. Add the garlic and ginger, stri-fry for 30secs then add the frankfurter
  5. Stir in the Kimchi, peas, spring onions and finally rice, leaving 30secs between each addition
  6. Separate the rice out well and fry for 3-4mins before pouring the sauce evenly all over
  7. Stri-fry for 1-2mins until all the liquid has been absorbed. Turn off the heat and stir in the sesame oil. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve
