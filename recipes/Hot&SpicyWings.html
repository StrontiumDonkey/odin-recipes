Hot & Spicy Chicken Wings with Maple Chipotle Hot Sauce

Description

Sweet, sticky and hot Chicken Wings you'll absolutely love!

Ingredients

  • 1kg Chicken Wings, tendon snipped at the joint and straightened out

For the spice rub

  • 1tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1tsp garlic granules
  • 1tsp celery salt
  • 1tsp golden caster sugar
  • 1tsp mustard powder
  • 1tbsp vegetable oil

For the hot sauce

  • 2tbsp cider vinegar
  • 2tsp smoked paprika
  • 1tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 3tbsp hot chilli sauce
  • 2tbsp maple syrup
  • 1tbsp chipotle paste

Method

  1. Heat oven to 200c/180c fan/Gas 6. Mix ingredients for spice rub together in a small bowl. Place chicken wings in a large bowl and cover with the spice rub and seasoning. Mix well. Spread out in a single layer on a large foil-lined baking tray. Roast for 45 mins, then remove and drain off excess oil.
  2. Mix the ingredients for the hot sauce and brush over the cooked wings. Return to the oven at 220c/200c fan/Gas 7 for 15 mins, brushing every few mins with extra sauce. Alternatively cook on direct heat barbecue for 3-4mins per side.
